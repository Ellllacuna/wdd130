Rafting Momentum

At Rafting Momentum, we are dedicated to providing an unforgettable whitewater rafting experience that brings adventure, camaraderie, and a deep appreciation for nature. Our mission is to safely guide individuals, families, and groups through thrilling, pristine rivers while fostering a respect for the environment and local communities. With expert guides, top-quality equipment, and a commitment to excellence, we strive to inspire a sense of adventure and connection to the outdoors in everyone we serve. Whether you’re a first-time rafter or an experienced adventurer, we aim to create lasting memories, one rapid at a time

History

Founded in 2002, Rafting Momentum began as a small, passionate group of river enthusiasts committed to sharing the thrill of whitewater rafting with adventurers of all levels. What started as a modest operation on The Snake river quickly grew into a trusted provider of exciting and safe rafting experiences.

Over the years, we’ve expanded our services to include a variety of rivers, expert guides, and top-of-the-line equipment. Throughout our journey, our focus has remained on safety, environmental stewardship, and providing exceptional outdoor adventures. Today, Rafting Momentum is proud to be a leader in the rafting community, offering unforgettable experiences to thousands of guests each year.
